## Why These Models Endure

- Mobile / on-site → no rent or inventory
- Emergency calls = instant high-margin demand
- Recurring revenue (subscriptions, contracts, maintenance plans)
- Natural barriers (licenses, trust, local relationships)
- Massive tech upside → AI scheduling, predictive alerts, PWAs, smart home integrations

## Future-Proof Plays (2026+)

- AI diagnostics, predictive maintenance, automated quoting
- Green / climate-resilient upgrades (energy efficiency, flood-proofing)
- Digital-first customer experience (online booking, real-time tracking, apps)
- Smart home / IoT bundles → higher LTV & stickiness

## How to Use This Repo

- Scan for ideas that match your skills & location
- Pick 1–2 → validate with 10 local conversations
- Start lean (solo + van) → focus on reviews + Google Business
- Layer tech early (AI chat, scheduling agents, PWAs)
- Contribute: add your trade, update costs, share wins/fails, PRs welcome
